Practice Writing Hiragana

Mastering Hiragana requires consistent practice. Use our interactive canvas to practice writing each character with the correct stroke order.

Focus on the shape and proportions of each character. Japanese writing places great importance on the balance and flow of each stroke.

Practice Tips

  • Start with basic characters before moving to dakuten
  • Practice daily for 15-20 minutes
  • Pay attention to stroke order and direction
  • Use grid paper to maintain proper proportions
  • Say the character aloud as you write it
