Practice Writing Hiragana

Hiragana is the foundation of Japanese writing. Mastering these characters will help you read and write basic Japanese words and sentences.

Hiragana characters have a flowing, cursive style. Focus on the curves and connections as you practice.

Practice Tips

  • Start with basic characters before moving to dakuten
  • Practice writing simple words like "ありがとう" (thank you)
  • Pay attention to stroke order and direction
  • Compare similar characters (れ vs ね, ぬ vs め)
  • Say the character aloud as you write it
