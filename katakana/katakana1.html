Practice Writing Katakana

Katakana is used for foreign words, names, and technical terms in Japanese. Mastering these characters will help you read modern Japanese texts and understand borrowed words from other languages.

Katakana characters have more angular shapes compared to Hiragana. Focus on the straight lines and sharp angles as you practice.

Practice Tips

  • Start with basic characters before moving to combinations
  • Practice writing loanwords like "コンピューター" (computer)
  • Pay attention to stroke order and direction
  • Compare similar characters (ソ vs ン, シ vs ツ)
  • Say the character aloud as you write it
